Coffee Shop Policies

Return and Refund Policy

Our coffee products are perishable goods and therefore all sales are final. We do not accept returns or offer refunds for coffee purchases. However, if you receive a damaged or incorrect order, please contact us within 7 days of receipt for assistance.

Shipping Policy

We ship our coffee products within 2-3 business days after receiving your order. Shipping times may vary depending on your location. We offer standard and expedited shipping options at checkout.

Quality Assurance

Our coffee is sourced from the finest beans and roasted to perfection. We ensure that each batch meets our high standards of quality. If you are not satisfied with the taste or quality of our coffee, please contact us for support.

Privacy Policy

We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. We do not share your data with third parties without your consent. For more information, please review our full privacy policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our policies, please contact us at info@coffeeshop.com.
